GAMBLING TECHNOLOGY


Using the blockchain to enable the gambling experience results in a low cost, efficient, secure and provably fair platform for all your gambling needs. PlaySmart will deliver guaranteed fair and secure products which are totally transparent to all parties.

SOVEREIGN IDENTITY

Identity and verification are the key to a safe and secure gambling experience. PlaySmart allow individual players to control their digital identity. Individuals can take back ownership of their personal information and enable them to store it within an immutable distributed ledger. Thus effectively mitigating the risk of identity theft which has been the greatest vulnerability to centralised databases over the last few decades.

COMPLIANCE AND REGULATION

A safe and secure gambling environment goes hand in hand with an embedded regulatory compliance framework.

PlaySmart is working with key regulators who are are at the forefront of ensuring appropriate regulatory frameworks for the ever changing world of blockchain, cryptocurrencies and gambling.

FAIR Play

No middle handles your money. Bets are settled with help of network consensus. In the process the valid participating nodes get rewarded as they take care of the trust element.

No account needed to place a bet. Transactions are processed from authorised wallets with full audit available on the blockchain
